What is Mac OS?

Mac OS is an operating system developed and exclusively used by Apple inc. It is very popular within school systems, with programmers, and with creatives. It has gained popularity due to it being the main alternative to Windows and its ease of use, for people that have used it for years. There are many benefits to Mac OS, but security and lack of bloat (useless programs pre-installed on computer) are things many people mention when they speak of Mac OS.

Pros and Cons

Benefits of Mac OS

  • Hardware and software created to compliment each other
  • Apple support is near unmatched
  • Integrated with the Apple ecosystem (iPhone, Apple Watch, iMessage, etc.)
  • Has a store with most programs easily available
  • Many high quality pre-installed applications
  • Some programs for creatives are exclusively on Mac OS
  • Apple prides itself on the privacy it gives its customers
  • Mac OS tends to be very secure from viruses

Downsides of Mac OS

  • Mac OS computers tend to be more expensive for similar specifications
  • Integrated with the Apple ecosystem
  • Some programs are not on Mac OS
  • Hardware selection limited
  • Steep learning curve for people transitioning from Windows

Who Should Use It?

  • Creatives (artists, photographers, animators)
  • Programmers tend to use Mac OS
  • People who believe they will need to use customer service often
