What is Windows?

The Windows operating system hardly needs an introduction at this point. Unless you are on a mobile device, chances are, you are using a Windows computer. Windows was created by the Microsoft Corporation and is now the most popular operating system by a large margin. Because of the ubiquity of the operating system most programs are on Windows. Windows is used by most businesses across the world as well, because of the amount of programs available on the operating system. Windows is the standard when it comes to operating systems. If you were to walk into a store today looking for a computer, your choices would include a couple different Mac OS computers and dozens of Windows computers made by many different companies in many different price ranges.

Pros and Cons

Benefits of Windows

  • Almost any program one needs is on Windows
  • Used in many workplaces
  • No learning needed by most people
  • Part of the Microsoft ecosystem
  • Many choices in hardware across a variety of prices and from many manufacturers
  • Touch screen compatible

Downsides of Windows

  • Many viruses target Windows due to its popularity
  • Part of Microsoft ecosystem
  • Lower end hardware can lead to a bad experience
  • Bloatware (applications people do not want) comes on most PCs purchased in stores

Who Should Use It?

  • Business Professionals
  • People who are comfortable with Windows
  • People in school (depending on the degree)
  • People in finance
  • Large corporations
